Lahore Hotel, Pakistan

The 42 guest rooms and seven elegant suites in this outstanding hotel in Pakistan range from the remarkably roomy Standard and Deluxe Rooms to the exquisitely furnished Jasmine, Heritage, and Royal Heritage Suites. Take a break from the sun by our outdoor pool, which features cosy loungers, poolside dining, and beverages. You can even play a game of badminton or table tennis.
Air-conditioned, tastefully decorated rooms and suites feature carpeting, a clothes rack, an in-room safe, and a resting space. There is also a minibar and a flat-screen satellite TV. The private bathrooms in the rooms have a shower, a hairdryer, slippers, and complimentary toiletries. The rooms also have garden views.The helpful staff can make arrangements for car rentals, laundry services, and luggage storage for visitors. Additionally offered are meeting/banquet facilities and an airport shuttle.
